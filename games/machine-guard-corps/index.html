Machine Guard Corps

Deploy machines from the left lane, outplay the alien economy, and keep the command base online.

  • Both sides gain energy, summon units, and upgrade their base during the fight.
  • Scrap from kills buys player upgrades. Alien tech improves itself over time.
  • Machine attacks build heat. At 100 heat, a unit shuts down for 2 seconds.
  • Both main towers fire automatically, but turret upgrades matter as pressure rises.
  • Fortresses slowly repair after 8 seconds without damage. Command Tower improves repair speed.
  • Boss kills award major Energy and Scrap, but the hive only falls when its core HP reaches zero.
  • Each machine line can buy one permanent evolution with Scrap; stronger evolutions cost more.
