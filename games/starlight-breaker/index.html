🌟 STARLIGHT BREAKER

An Artistic Brick Breaking Experience

🎮 HOW TO PLAY

🖱️ Move your mouse to control the deer paddle

📱 On mobile: Touch and drag to move the paddle

⭐ Bounce the star ball to break all bricks

💖 You have 3 lives - don't let the ball fall!

🎯 Clear all bricks to win the game

⌨️ Press ESC to pause/resume the game
