HOW TO PLAY

You deliver burgers on foot for Borgir — ever since rat-people tore the cataclysmic converter out of your car (or so the boss says). You dream of a Yellow Card: a licence to drive a real Violencetown taxi. First, get the car running.

Move & interact

  • W A S D or arrows — walk one tile, hold to keep going
  • Tap / click the ground to walk there (paths around buildings)
  • Tap / click a target → walk over & do the obvious thing (Take / Talk / Attack); long-press or right-click for all options · F opens that list for who you face

Combat & actions

  • Space opens the action wheel anywhere — it pre-aims at the nearest enemy. Double-tap Space to instantly repeat your last action.
  • On the wheel: ↑ ↓ pick a ring (action · item · direction) · ← → spin it · Space fire · Esc back out toward walking
  • On touch: tap ✦ to open the wheel; tap any slice to set it (the outer ring is a direction compass); tap the center, or ✦ again, to fire

Inventory

  • 1–9 select a slot · Space opens its use overlay · pick Use / Smash / Give with arrows (throwing is on the action wheel)
  • On touch: tap a hotbar slot to select, tap again to open the overlay, tap an option

Other

  • T wait one turn · Esc cancel any menu · ? open this help
  • L open the log history · On touch: tap the log strip
  • E examine what you're facing (the car, signs, oddities)
  • Tab open the Remoticon (ITEMS · GEAR · QUESTS · MAP); [ ] switch tabs; C/J/M jump to GEAR/QUESTS/MAP · On touch: tap the ▤ button
  • On touch: ☰ opens the menu (Wait / Cancel / Options / Help / Restart)

Autosaves locally — CONTINUE on the splash resumes your run. Code at github.com/RussellDangerr/violencetown.
